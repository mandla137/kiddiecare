About Us

Profile

History

Founded in 2017, Kiddie Care Academy has been a school which primarily had its focus on pre-school level of education. However, this has not limited expansion, as the school now caters for both pre-school and primary level of education. The school was founded and is run by Mr and Mrs Makhubela, who are both very passionate about education and the right nurturing and upbringing of children.

Our Mission

To become the leading academic institution within and beyond the borders of Lesotho, offering the very best education to the young generation while also maintain the highest degree of education standard

Our Vision

To maintain integrity and high moral standards
To serve the students as if they were our own
To protect the talents and uniqueness of eachstudent
To provide a comofortable, safe and most ideal learning environment at all times

Our Team

Expert teachers

When choosing teachers to join our team, we carefully consider the qualifications of such individuals. We ensure that the teachers who end up becoming a part of us are well qualified academically to carryout the tasks, responsibilities, as well as duties assigned to them. This suffices to note that we are a very strict institution when it comes to the selection criteria of our staff.

Professionals

Proficiency is imperative and very key in all operations of the school. We therefore emphasize this to our staff, in order to maintain the professional standards we are well known for across the country and beyond. Our staff is professional in operations, conduct, and in communication, which is why we always make everyone who sets foot at our institute feel like they are home away from home.

Full package

We carefully take our time when it comes to selecting our staff, because we aim at acquiring professional experts, who have not lost touch with their fun bone. This we do because our school believes that there is a child in everyone, big or small, boththe young and the old, and it brings such delight to us when we see that child inside being brought to life and let to shine optimally.

Provident

The upbringing of a child is one of the major factor which contribute towards the betterment of a better world of tomorrow, as thus we put in all of our effort to ensure that every child enrolled with us is groomed and developed in to a better person regardless of the career path he or she will follow once at tertiary.

Productive

We measure our success rate by using productivity as one of the determining factors. Our team therefore consists of productive individuals who always work towards attaining great milestones. This inevitably rubs off on the students, leading them towards becoming productive persons who are progressive.

Loving and Caring

Our team consists of individuals who understands that every child enrolled with us should be treated like our very own. This meaning that we treat all students equally, with love, respect, kindness, utmost belief and nurturing all of them towards becoming great leaders of the morrow.

Educational trip

By: Mrs Ts'epy Makubela Amoako

As part of our annual culture, we will embark on an educational trip, which will head to Thaba Bosiu. The trip will entail tour guidance, educate students about the rich history of our beautiful country, and also become part of the recreational program. The trip will cost LSL 450.00 and this amount includes three meals for the kids. Parents are therefore strongly encouraged to ensure that children become part of such a trip.
